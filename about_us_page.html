Welcome to THE EATERY-A Culinary Jounery Beyond Boundaries

At THE EATERY, we believe that dining is not just about the food on your plate; it's an immersive experience that engages all the senses. Established in 2010, our restaurant has been a beacon of culinary excellence, where passion for gastronomy meets warm hospitality.

Takeaway Treasures

For those on the go, our takeaway service ensures that you can savor our culinary creations wherever you are. Enjoy the same quality and flavor in the convenience of your own space.

Our Culinary History

Founded by Mr.Siriwardhana, THE EATERY has a rich history rooted in a passion for food and a commitment to excellence. What began as a humble eatery has evolved into a culinary destination known for its fusion of flavors, innovative techniques, and a dedication to customer satisfaction.
